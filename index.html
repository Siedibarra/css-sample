About Us

This platform is intended to learn and improve situational awareness and skills of an aspiring Habal-habal driver. As well as to know the basic Do's and Dont's in any severe situations.

Mission

Our mission is to provide each student with the necessary information and techniques to be a SAFE RESPONSIBLE DRIVER.


To provide a learning friendly environment that meets the needs of everyone's learning style, and to provide the motoring public with RESPONSIBLE DRIVERS

Vision

We envision a world where road users, pedestrians and motorists alike, would share the road and follow the rules on the road.


We Aim provide the world with SAFE RESPONSIBLE DRIVERS to maintain a RISK AND INJURY FREE ENVIRONMENT.
